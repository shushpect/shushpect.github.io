Evergreen Mortgage

Evergreen Mortgage is an equity based private lender. They believe that “affordability” isn’t always dictated by the debt ratio, and the credit worthiness shouldn’t be equal to the credit situation.

Through the metaphor of pine cone, we were able to convey the concept of “safe growth”, stability and the local vibe of British Columbia.

The new website is clean but informative, calm but engaging, simple but flexible. It also reflects the work put into the brand strategy: brand touchpoints becoming call-to-actions, audience's mindsets becoming usage scenarios, etc.
