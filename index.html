The framework

The approach is mainly based on marketing studies performed by Byron Sharp, Peter Field and Les Binet. The process is also shaped by the principles of Outcome-Driven Innovation and storytelling.

1. Defining the job

We start with defining the job of the customer. Unlike personas, jobs-to-be-done let us target all people interested in a specific outcome. And let us invent better solutions to fulfill their goals.

2. Mapping competitors

We compare existing solutions to find opportunity windows for distinctiveness. It is primarily about the stylistic direction, tone of voice, color palette, and metaphor (legend, mental model).

3. Designing brand identity

We design a brand system and its assets. Distinctive style, voice, and metaphor form the concept of identity. Colors, typography, other verbal and graphic assets become the consistent tools.
