Living Mirror

Living Mirror is a “playback” theater with improv performances based on the stories of the viewers. It brings focus to their own lives, helps to connect on the basis of shared experiences.

According to the concept, playback is both a mirror for our stories and a portal to the world of feelings. Minimalist, clean layouts help to lower the costs of maintaining the identity.
