Logofolio

Distinctive logos that are built to last. They don’t depend on trends or media restrictions such as color modes, scale and composition.

5-Eleven Hoops

5-Eleven Hoops is a basketball center for children with autism. The infinity symbol depicts both autism and hoops. Little dots at 5 and 11 o’clock make it possible to copyright the mark separately.

CityUpdate

CityUpdate is a social initiative and an urbanists’ movement. It got a contemporary Modernism-inspired logo that is just midway “C” and “U”, and looks like the bird’s-eye view of a building.

Evergreen Mortgage

Evergreen Mortgage is an equity based mortgage lender. The pine cone mark symbolizes protected, risk-free growth.

Franco

Franco is a construction company specializing in concrete buildings. It got a logo that is made of concrete forms.

Greenverd

Greenverd is an eco-friendly online store that is all about gradual evolution rather than revolution. So there is an arrow in the letter “G” to depict upright movement.

Inspiration Family

Inspiration Family is a cancer foundation. It got a cozy and joyful logo that depicts different stages of the cell’s growth. And they also form the letters “I” and “F”.

Living Mirror

Living Mirror is a playback theater, an improv theater “reflecting” the audience’s stories. Thus, the letter “O” is shaped like a mirror.

Macramé

Macramé is a dating service. So the logo looks both like macramé and like people holding hands.

Mitipi

Mitipi is a smart insurance company. The logo depicts the triangular shape of the key product, and also shows its main feature — projecting light.

Skole

Skole is a Ukrainian touristic town. Following the meaning of the name (which is similar to “split, cleaved”), the top parts of the logo are chopped off with a digital axe.
