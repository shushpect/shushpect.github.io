Holodomor

The Holodomor was a famine deliberately organized by the USSR in 1932—1933. It killed millions of Ukrainians. There is a tradition now to light a candle as a sign of remembrance of the tragedy.

Inspiration Family is a Ukrainian cancer foundation. Unlike many other organizations of the field, Inspiration Family focuses not on tragedy and pity, but on empowerment and community.
